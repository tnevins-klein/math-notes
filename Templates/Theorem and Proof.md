> [!INFO] Theorem
> Let $a$ be an arbitrary constant.
> $$\lim_{x \to a} x = a$$

---

> [!NOTE] Proof
> Let $\varepsilon > 0$. Choose $\delta = \varepsilon$. Thus,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> 	|x - a| < \delta \quad &\text{by assumption}\\
> 	|x - a| < \varepsilon \quad &\text{by def. of $\delta$}
> \end{align}
> $$
> $\square$
